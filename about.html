About MavenCore Water Technologies

At MavenCore Water Technologies, we are dedicated to delivering expert water treatment solutions with clarity, simplicity, and exceptional service. As a newly established company in the water treatment sector, our mission is to take the complexity out of compliance and system design — making water treatment straightforward and stress-free for end users, contractors, and consultants alike.

We Specialise In:

  • Scale Control – including advanced water softening and conditioning technologies
  • Bacterial Control – featuring systems like copper-silver ionisation, UV disinfection, and chlorine dioxide treatment

Our Consultancy Services

Beyond our technical solutions, we offer specialist consultancy services to advise on water safety strategies and compliance best practices. Whether it’s supporting your design process or ensuring your systems align with health and safety regulations, we provide clear, actionable guidance that protects your operations and reputation.

Customer-First Approach

With a customer-first mindset, we eliminate technical jargon and deliver tailored solutions for various sectors — ensuring system efficiency, regulatory compliance, and long-term water quality.
